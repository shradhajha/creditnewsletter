Credit Team Highlights: Grit, Guts, and Great Outcomes!

Our Credit Analysts continue to go above and beyond for clients, solving complex scenarios, pushing through difficult cases, and making a meaningful difference. Here are some powerful wins shared by our team:

Silvia: Super Strategy & Leave Win

AMP approval despite income complexity: Clients relied on rental and super income. Silvia leveraged Income Protection Payments and managed a tricky structure where AMP wouldn't accept OOP as security, so the equity was used to purchase OOP fully. The deal settled smoothly, and the clients were delighted.

Land loan success during bereavement leave: The client was on bereavement leave but had cleverly aligned Centrelink and annual leaves. Despite back-and-forth and near-loss of hope, Silvia pushed through and got the land loan settled.

Learning: Creative structuring and emotional intelligence go a long way in tough deals.

Aishwarya: Compassion in Action

Client's emotional refinance journey: A long-term client, recently widowed, struggled with repayments. Despite poor credit, Aishwarya and Team Amine fought to retain the loan with AMP. With sensitivity and transparency, they consolidated debts, secured a competitive rate, and gave Rohit much-needed financial relief. He was deeply grateful‚Äîa story of compassion and commitment.

Learning: Emotional intelligence is as important as credit skill in building trust with vulnerable clients.

Suraj: Crypto Crisis Averted

Crypto recovery rescue: A client facing financial ruin due to crypto losses had one repayment left before default. Suraj structured a low-doc solution by extending the term and using equity. Multiple Live Chats, consistent follow-ups, and a creative structure saved the client's home.

Learning: Fast thinking and flexibility with product structures can save the day.

Urusha: Score Play Pays Off

Credit score challenge solved: A client's Equifax score showed as 575, but the comprehensive score was 604. Urusha verified with AOL (which showed 815) and confidently submitted to Westpac, knowing they prioritize the comprehensive score. The deal sailed through without issue.

Learning: Understanding lender preferences around credit scores can be the key to success.

Rabin: Payday Cleanup Hero

Breaking the payday loan cycle: A client was drowning in payday loans and at risk of losing their name. After multiple lender rejections and hardship complications, Rabin found a solution with VMG that required just one full mortgage repayment‚Äîno hardship exit needed. The deal settled, debts consolidated, and the client's monthly cash flow improved by $1800. A truly life-changing result.

Learning: Even the most complex hardship cases can be turned around with a strong lender match.

Rojina: RSU Relay Victory

RSU exception triumph: The client had no RSU income in 2023. After multiple exceptions with Macquarie, extensive follow-ups from Jan to Feb 2025, and policy changes mid-way, Rojina recalculated RSU income three times. She secured two RSU exceptions and a pre-approval despite missed documents‚Äîa huge win after months of persistence.

Learning: Persistence and policy mastery make the difference in exception-heavy files.

RSU win: Finsit RSU income loan formally approved with Bankwest! Elisha reached out to higher credit for exception approval. The deal involved a refinance, equity release, and a pre-approval for investment, using a combination of salary RSU income bonus.

Learning: Don't hesitate to escalate‚Äîproactive exception requests can secure big wins.

Gunjan: SMSF Trust Masterclass

SMSF loan approved through creative servicing: Got SMSF deal settled using recent payslip contributions even though it didn't meet the 12 month rule. Also supported a complex investment purchase under trust with expat guarantor, tweaking LODs to meet the client's legal needs‚Äîone-shot approval.

Learning: A deep grasp of servicing policies helps bend the rules ethically and effectively.
